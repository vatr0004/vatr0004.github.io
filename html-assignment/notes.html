5 HTML Tags

<p>
Paragraph: This tag helps to write text on your webpage. It is often followed by </p> once the writing has been finalized for the section.
<body>
Body: This tag contains the body of the page that your viewers can see. Everything inserted in this tag will be visible to those navigating your website. It is followed by </body> once all of the content desired to be seen has been inserted into the webpage.
<title>
Title: We can showcase the title of the webpage by using this tag. Following it with </title> once your desired title has been inserted.
<strong>
Strong: This tag represents a strong text, it bolds the letters into your webpage. </strong> is used to enclose the tag.
<html>
HTML Document: This tag represents the html document iself, it's inserted before the body. </html> is used to close the tag, after all of the information has been applied to the webpage.
